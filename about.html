  • Analyzing Bids available on various government portals and classifying them by sector, target audience, size of opportunity and other factors.
  • Collating and analyzing relevant information to support bid development including in-depth analysis of business performance.
  • Conducting research via a wide variety of industry and data sources.
  • Developing complex models to assess and forecast business performance
  • Working with colleagues to evaluate business strategy
  • Drawing conclusions from findings and communicating ideas and recommendations through written and verbal presentations, and
  • Supporting the preparation of bid documents.

Bid Writer

Our Bid Writers work under the supervision of the Bid Manager and assist them in developing a proposal structure. They help translate the technical language of the solution team and SMEs into easy-to-understand business language for your target customer. They specialize in writing effective, concise and compelling technical content, while managing the strict timelines. They not only write technical content of the bids, but also create presentations and supporting documentation, ensuring a cohesive and consistent approach. Our Bid writers are adept at:

  • Writing proposal responses based on RFP/RFQ instruction and detailed information from subject matter experts (SMEs) and knowledge holders, referring back to them for editing and final approval, leading to increased success in winning contracts
  • Understanding the win themes and baking them effectively in different areas of the response document.
  • Effectively highlighting the value proposition and solution elements that make a winning proposal
  • Collating the input of disparate contributions to create a coherent and well-structured final response
  • Proof-reading, editing and structuring the response document so it looks professional and meets the utmost quality standards.
  • Ensuring the timely creation and review of all bid collaterals
  • Package the solution in a manner that will support their marketing and attractiveness
  • preparing response documents and presentations in the necessary formats and liaising with creative team to design relevant and effective graphics and style sheet templates.

Bid Manager

The Bid Manager runs the end-to-end proposal process as a project. He/she will co-ordinate and produce the proposal response by identifying and assembling a bid team with the relevant knowledge and expertise needed to win. This is a critical role that requires a dynamic personality with the ability to work under pressure, stick to challenging deadlines and network across multiple teams and platforms. Our accomplished Bid Managers fit the above requirements to the letter. They are highly motivated, innovative and above all, have a professional approach to the Bid Management process. They pride themselves on their attention to detail, excellent communication skills and effectiveness in delivering high-quality bids time and time again. Their primary responsibilities include:

  • Own the end-to-end bid process.
  • Identify and bring together the right Bid team for the bid underway.
  • Understand and analyze the Bid. Create a proposal outline and brief the team using this outline.
  • Organize and run Win theme, story-boarding & key message workshops and ensure the resulting messages are baked into the written proposal.
  • Organize and run in-depth Solution workshops to ensure the most appropriate and relevant solutions for each bid are effectively pulled together for the response.
  • Compile, edit, and proof read proposal responses and ensure that these are presented in a professional and concise manner.
  • Review and revise documents prepared by others and coordinate the preparation of those materials in a completed bid document.
  • Create a Bid schedule for internal deadline and reviews, identify risks and build in redundancies to ensure that all documentation preparation and reviews are tracked and completed well within your customer deadline.
  • Develop reference materials and proper documentation to assist in transfer of control bac to you after the Bid process is over.
  • Do post Bid Analysis and document lessons learned from the Bid
